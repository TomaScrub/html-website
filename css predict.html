#974 - Cogmantle

  • Type: Steel / Fighting
  • Height: 1.5 m
  • Weight: 98.3 kg

Cogmantle plans its battles with machine-like precision, analysing terrain and opponents in moments. It disables foes with counters and calculated strikes, rarely wasting movement. Some Trainers report it rearranging their gear for “optimal efficiency”.

  • Battle Analyst - Opponents' dodge has no effect on likelihood to hit.
  • Gear Strike - Attacks opponents with a spinning cog, which increases in power each round it doesn't hit.
